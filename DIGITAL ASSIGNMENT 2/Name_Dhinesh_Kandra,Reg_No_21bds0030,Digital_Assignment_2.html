Pairplot For Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) :

Inference For Pairplot For Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) :

Bar Plot to show the total crashes by states in United states :

In [7]:
plt.figure(figsize=(12, 6))
sns.barplot(x='abbrev', y='total', data=crashes, errorbar=None)
plt.xticks(rotation=90)
plt.xlabel('State Abbreviation')
plt.ylabel('Total Crashes')
plt.title('Total Crashes by State')
plt.show()

Inference for Bar Plot :

Identifying the states with the lowest and highesh total crashes for example: South Carolina has high crash rates, while Vermont has low crash rates.

Scatter Plot to visualize alcohol consumption and its relation with total crashes :

In [8]:

Inference for Scatter Plot :
